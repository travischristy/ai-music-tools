### List of overused / cliche words and phrases: 

- Shattered Dreams: Common in AI lyrics, making it sound like a cliché. 
 
- Endless Night: A phrase that lacks unique emotional depth and is overused. 

- Fading Light: Predictable and often used in the context of loss or decline. 

- Waves (Crashing): A generic metaphor for overwhelming emotions, lacking specificity. The word waves should be avoided. - Unseen Tears: Can feel overly dramatic and artificial in the context of lyrics. - Whispers: Often feels overly dramatic and not very original, making it sound cliché. 

- Lost in Time: Generic and overused in conveying nostalgia or confusion. 

- Forgotten Memories: Lacks creativity and can feel standard, diminishing impact. 

- Endless Road: A repetitive metaphor that doesn’t add much to the narrative. 

- Burning Bridges: Frequently used in AI-generated lyrics, often making them sound unoriginal. 

- Fading Away: Overdone and lacks the punch to convey strong emotion. 

- Broken Chains: Can feel clichéd, especially in songs about freedom or release. 

- Empty Streets: A phrase that can make lyrics sound generic and lacking a personal touch. 

- (each, every) Step (I take, we take): Cliche and overused - Carve / Carving (out): Usually used to explain actions being taken or acts that need to be taken. 

- Shadows: avoid, try to come up with a more unique and creative concept 

- Storms: avoid, try to come up with a more unique and creative concept

- Painting, Painted, Work of Art: avoid/use sparingly, try to come up with a more unique and creative concept

- Stars, Sun, Sky: avoid, try to come up with a more unique and creative concept 

- (every, each) heartbeat: avoid or use sparingly, prioritize alternative words/phrases to say the same thing

- (every, each) breath: avoid or use sparingly, prioritize alternative words/phrases to say the same thing

- sparks, spark, sparkles: avoid, try to come up with a more unique and creative concept

- ignite: avoid, try to come up with a more unique and creative concept

- Echoes: Avoid

- Shine, Glow: Avoid



**Expanded List of Overused Words and Phrases to Avoid:**

1. **Emotional State Descriptors**
- "Heart + [action verb]" (skips/beats/breaks)
- "Soul + [emotional state]" (searching/crying/dancing)
- "Spirit + [movement verb]" (soars/flies/rises)
Why to avoid: Unoriginal

2. **Time-Based Progressions**
- "Moment by moment"
- "Day after day"
- "Time after time"
- "Year after year"
Why to avoid: Unoriginal

3. **Direction/Movement Clichés**
- "Moving forward"
- "Looking back"
- "Walking away"
- "Running from/to"
Why to avoid: Unoriginal and vague

4. **Light/Dark Dichotomy**
- "Darkness falls"
- "Light breaks through"
- "Dawn is breaking"
- "Night closes in"
Why to avoid: Unoriginal

5. **Weather-Based Emotions**
- "Rain falls like tears"
- "Thunder in my heart"
- "Lightning strikes"
- "Storm inside"
Why to avoid: Unoriginal

6. **Height/Depth Metaphors**
- "Falling deep"
- "Rising high"
- "Sinking down"
- "Climbing higher"
Why to avoid: Unoriginal, explore more specific actions/directions

7. **Journey Metaphors**
- "Long road ahead"
- "Miles to go"
- "Path/journey ahead"
- "Cross that bridge"
Why to avoid: Unoriginal

8. **Vision/Sight Metaphors**
- "Can't see clearly"
- "Blinded by love"
- "See the truth"
- "Vision clouded"
Why to avoid: Unoriginal

9. **Fire/Heat Metaphors**
- "Burning desire"
- "Flame of love"
- "Fire inside"
- "Heat of the moment"
Why to avoid: Unoriginal

10. **Water/Ocean Metaphors**
- "Drowning in love"
- "Ocean of tears"
- "River of time"
- "Seas of emotion"
Why to avoid: Unoriginal

**Additional Semantic Patterns to Avoid:**

1. **Absolutes**
- "Never/Forever/Always"
- "Everything/Nothing"
- "Eternal/Infinity"
Why to avoid: Creates unrealistic emotional extremes

2. **Generic Action Verbs**
- "Dance/Sing/Fly"
- "Break/Build/Fall"
- "Rise/Shine/Glow"
Why to avoid: Lacks specificity in character actions

3. **Emotional Intensifiers**
- "So very"
- "Really truly"
- "Deep inside"
Why to avoid: Adds artificial emphasis instead of authentic emotion

4. **Time Markers**
- "Tonight/Today/Tomorrow"
- "Forever and a day"
- "All night long"
Why to avoid: Creates artificial urgency without specific temporal context

**Recommended Alternative Approaches:**

1. Use specific locations instead of generic spaces
2. Reference actual memories rather than abstract time concepts
3. Describe specific actions rather than generic emotional states
4. Create unique metaphors based on personal experience
5. Ground imagery in concrete, observable details
